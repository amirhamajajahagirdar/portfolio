About

Learning through real projects.

I am Amir Hamaja Jahagirdar, a student developer building a strong foundation in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Git, GitHub, and PHP. I enjoy turning ideas into working projects because building helps me understand concepts more deeply than only watching tutorials.

My goal is to become a Full Stack Developer and later specialize in Artificial Intelligence. I believe consistent improvement matters more than perfection, and every project teaches something new about design, logic, debugging, and real-world problem solving.

I am building my portfolio publicly as proof of progress. Each application I create is a step toward cleaner code, stronger thinking, better user experiences, and the confidence to solve meaningful problems with technology.
